To add a line-through effect on the original price (M.R.P), you can use the <s> (strikethrough) tag or apply textDecoration: "line-through" in CSS.


<Card.Text>
  <h3 style={{ color: "green" }}> ₹ {item.price}/- Only </h3>
</Card.Text>
<Card.Text>
  <h5 style={{ color: "red" }}> M.R.P: <s>₹ {item.originalPrice}</s> ({item.discount}% off)</h5>
</Card.Text>
